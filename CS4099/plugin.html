For ease of use I implemented the script as form of a Google Chrome browser extension. The decision to utilise Google Chrome was at the request of the industry contact and as Google Chrome has the largest browser market share by a significant proportion which is shown here. The extension first highlights all IFrame objects within a web page by placing a red border around them. Once the browser extension button is clicked, the IFrame content will then be replaced with both id and src of the IFrame.
